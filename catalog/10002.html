Inventory of environmental citizen science projects - Annex: Survey results

Publisher: European Commission, Joint Research Centre (JRC)

Data collected through an online survey, to complement the environmental citizen science project inventory that resulted from the desk research study ('Study on an inventory of citizen science activities for environmental policies') ordered by the European Commission (DG ENV, with the support of DG JRC) in order to get an evidence base of citizen science activities.
